Cryss' Greenhouses

Three greenhouses, fully navmeshed (DLC required) (small, medium, large) I wanted to use with Horizon with a Winter playtrough. All three have a concret foundation for easier placement on unleveled ground.

I didn't add any decoration so you have plenty of room to let your inner architect run wild!

This is my first mod and there may be bugs. Please report so I can fix it and learn from it.

The Greenhouses can be found in Prefabs/Warehouses

Post your pictures and show us how you make use of them and have them decorated!
