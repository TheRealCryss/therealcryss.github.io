Cryss' Interiors - Apartments

[Under development]

What was the original idea for this mod? See this journal entry.

This mod is the first in my endavour to create my own collection of new interiors for Fallout 4.

Apartments adds

Fast Travel Point:

coc CryssInteriorsApartments

Current status:

  • Staircase, 3 doors to the exterior (maybe put something on the balconies in future updates)
  • A mini quest involving a cat
  • A useable cooking station in one of the apartments
  • A resident, somewhat

Todos:

  • I havn't decided upon the lighting, yet. Maye just some lights repairable with a spare battery
  • Open up more rooms and connect adjacent buildings
  • Sewer access (possible FROST in mind)
