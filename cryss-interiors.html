Cryss' Interiors

[Under development / v0.2.4-beta release]

What was the original idea for this mod? See this journal entry.

This mod is the first in my endavour to create my own collection of new interiors for Fallout 4.

Thanks to Terragon and Evelin for their helpful feedback and thourough testing.

Cryss Interiors contains the following new interior cells:

Apartments:

Close to vendor Slim

Fast Travel Point:

coc CryssInteriorsApartments

Concord Garage

Fast Travel Point:

coc CryssInteriorsCGarage

Waystation Storeroom

South of Boston when you follow the railway tracks

Fast Travel Point:

coc CryssInteriorsWaystationStoreroom

In development:
Loft

Near Goodneighbor/Mass Fusion building up some stairs

Fast Travel Point:

coc CryssInteriorsLoft

Where to get:

Github
