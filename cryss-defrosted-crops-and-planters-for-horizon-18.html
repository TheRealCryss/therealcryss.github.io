Cryss' Defrosted Crops and Planters for Horizon 1.8

[unmaintained]

To increase immersion when doing a Horizon winter playthrough and restrict myself to only grow crops in my greenhouses I was frustrated by the fact, that all crops I place are snow covered. So I made this patch. It is my second mod now and I hope everything is working for you.

Please note:
As I'm still earning to use CK and Xedit I couldn't find a smarter way of "restoring" the non-snow textures for specific items by just excluding them from the overwriting snow texture mod (I'm using Fallout 4 Seasons) I made a subfolder with renamed vanilla textures and bgms that I selected for every Horizon specific item like the automatic planters.

This is the first release and therefore there may be some inconsistencies or items that I have overlooked.

Thanks to Greb for the critical bugfix that was in my first release (0.5). (I better double check what CK does in the future. Lesson learned!). Get this version 1.0!

Requirements:

  • Horizon 1.8
